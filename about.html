About Me

Independent, Creative, Hard Worker & Learner

As an independent person, there is never a word of giving up in my dictionary. Always to work hard, be responsible for all the tasks that I carry out and trying to be creative to find solutions to every problem.

As long as I'm alive, I will continue to learn, because to survive we must adapt and evolve.

"Seek knowledge when you are poor, it will be your treasure. When you are rich, it will be your jewelry."





Cats Lovers

I have a soft soul. As social beings, we must love and care for every living creature, not only humans, but also animals and the environment around us.

I really love cats, in my opinion cats are the cutest, gentlest animals and relieve fatigue after a day of activities.





Healthy Lifestyle Lovers

I realized that I am no longer young. As a single mom, I have to take care of my physical and mental health. Because there is more responsibility on my shoulders, apart from taking care of myself, I also have to take care of my children.

Good and healthy food and drink is important to me. Watching movies at the cinema is also a way to maintain mental health. Never stop moving, even if it's just light exercise at home or just going for a morning walk outside.





An Adventurer

Traveling the world is one way to gain knowledge and life experience. I really like adventure, like visiting new places and learning about other cultures in new places. For me the experience of visiting a new place is a treasure that I can later pass on to my children as a story. I hope to inspire them. And they can adventure on their own when they grow up in the future.

I have a dream to wander abroad. "Knowledgeable and civilized people do not rest in their hometown. Leave your country and live a foreign life (in other people's land)."
